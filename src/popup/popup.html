How to Use This Extension

Search Modes:
  • Text Only: Search for text anywhere on the page
  • Links Aware: Highlight entire links containing your text
  • URL IDs: Find links by ID numbers in their URLs

Tips:
  • Enter multiple terms separated by commas or new lines
  • Each term gets a unique color (up to 8)
  • Your searches are saved for the session
  • Use URL IDs mode for finding items by their ID numbers
